About NextWave

Founded by a dedicated aquatics professional with over a decade of experience across lifeguarding, pool management, instruction, and operational leadership, NextWave Aquatic Training combines real-world expertise with nationally recognized certification programs.

We’ve successfully trained and supervised teams from small community pools to major aquatic centers, with a focus on safety, professionalism, and team cohesion. Whether you’re an aspiring lifeguard, a facility manager, or an organization seeking turnkey training solutions, we bring the skills and structure to help you succeed.
